The Best Movies According to Riddhima

My top 3 movies of all-time:

  1. "Hereditary" (2018)

    2. This is my favourite psychological horror film.

  2. "The Fault in Our Stars" (2014)

    3. This movie beautifully narrates the poignant love story of two teenagers. Together they navigate their illnesses, dreams, and the profound experiences of first love.

  3. "Now You See Me" (2013)

    4. It is a heist thriller movie that revolves around a group of illusionists who perform bank heists during their shows,blending magic with crime and mystery.
